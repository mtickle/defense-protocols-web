:::danger[Stop!]
If you are currently feeling the urge to harm yourself, or if you are planning suicide, please stop reading and seek help immediately.
:::

This manual is for spiritual support, but it is not a substitute for emergency medical or psychological intervention. If you are in crisis, you need a human voice right now.

* In the US: Dial or Text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline).
* Emergency: Dial 911.
* Global: Go to findahelpline.com to find a crisis center in your country.

The enemy wants you isolated. The bravest thing you can do right now is let someone in.

Make the call.
